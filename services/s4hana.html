Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an S/4HANA migration take?

Brownfield 4–8 months; Greenfield 9–15 months; SDT 6–12 months depending on scope, code and change management.

Which approach is right for us?

Choose Brownfield for speed with minimal change, Greenfield for redesign and standardisation, SDT when consolidating or selectively transforming.

How do you ensure a clean core?

We externalise custom logic to SAP BTP, use standard APIs and keep modifications upgrade‑safe to reduce run costs and future risk.
