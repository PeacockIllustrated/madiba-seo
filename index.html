SAP S/4 HANA Migration – Common Questions

  • Mid-market programmes typically run 6–12 months; enterprise migrations are phased over 12–24+ months depending on data, integrations, geographies, and change/training needs.

  • RISE targets established SAP customers consolidating to cloud with one contract; GROW suits new/smaller footprints starting greenfield with prescriptive scope.

  • Public = faster value, standardised processes, SaaS economics. Private = greater control, customisation, and complex/regulatory integrations.

  • Data volume/quality, custom-code remediation, number of integrations, multi-country rollout, non-functional requirements, and change & training effort.
